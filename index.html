About Me

The Art of Seeing

I'm Alexander Kane, a photographer driven by the belief that every moment holds extraordinary beauty — if you know where to look. Over twelve years, I've refined my craft across wedding photography, editorial portraiture, fashion campaigns, and documentary storytelling.

My approach blends technical mastery with raw emotional instinct. I don't just capture images; I create visual narratives that resonate deeply with the human experience. From the intimate whisper of a first look to the grandeur of a fashion editorial, every frame is composed with intention.

Based in New York, I travel worldwide to work with clients who value artistry, authenticity, and the power of a single photograph to tell an entire story.

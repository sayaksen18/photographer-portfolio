About Me

The Art of Seeing

I'm Alexander Kane, a photographer driven by the belief that every moment holds extraordinary beauty — if you know where to look. Over twelve years, I've refined my craft across wedding photography, editorial portraiture, fashion campaigns, and documentary storytelling.

My approach blends technical mastery with raw emotional instinct. I don't just capture images; I create visual narratives that resonate deeply with the human experience. From the intimate whisper of a first look to the grandeur of a fashion editorial, every frame is composed with intention.

Based in New York, I travel worldwide to work with clients who value artistry, authenticity, and the power of a single photograph to tell an entire story.

How It Works

The Journey

From our first conversation to the final delivery, every step is crafted to ensure a seamless and extraordinary experience.

01

Consultation

We begin with a personal conversation to understand your vision, style preferences, and the story you want to tell.

02

Planning

Together, we design a detailed shot list, scout locations, coordinate styling, and create a timeline for your session.

03

The Session

This is where the magic happens. I guide you naturally through the session, capturing authentic moments and stunning compositions.

04

Editing & Curation

Each image is meticulously edited and color-graded to achieve a cinematic, timeless aesthetic unique to your story.

05

Delivery

Your final gallery is delivered through a beautiful online portal. Premium prints and albums are available upon request.

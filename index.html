About Me

ART TO ARTISAN

I'm Shirshak Pal, a photographer from Chandannagar—often known as the City of Lights.

How could I be born in a city of lights and not spend my life learning how to shape it?

Photography began as curiosity. A way of holding on to moments that would otherwise slip away. Over time, that curiosity became passion, and that passion became craft.

Today, whether I'm documenting a crowded concert, a forgotten street, or a mountain trail disappearing into the clouds, I'm chasing the same thing—stories hidden in light, emotion, and fleeting moments.

Based in West Bengal, India, I use photography and filmmaking to document journeys, experiences, and the people who make them meaningful—one frame at a time.
